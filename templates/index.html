Understanding our AI-powered heart disease prediction technology

AI Model Information

Machine Learning Algorithm

Our model uses advanced ensemble methods combining logistic regression, random forest, and gradient boosting algorithms to provide accurate risk assessments.

Training & Validation

Trained on thousands of clinical cases with rigorous cross-validation to ensure reliability and generalizability across diverse populations.

Data Source

UCI Heart Disease Dataset

Based on the renowned UCI Machine Learning Repository Heart Disease Dataset, collected from Cleveland Clinic Foundation, Hungarian Institute of Cardiology, and other leading medical institutions.

Clinical Parameters

Analyzes 13 key clinical features including demographics, chest pain characteristics, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, ECG results, and exercise stress test parameters.
